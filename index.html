About Meraki Films

Meraki in greek means to do something with passion, with absolute devotion, with undivided attention. Meraki Films is founded in 2022 with an intention to bring global ideas and innovation to Indian movies. We believe movies have an undeniable impact on shaping human thinking process and culture of a society. We strive to entertain and influence masses with our movies.
